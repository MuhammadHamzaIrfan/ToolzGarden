What This Tool Does

Have you ever wondered why web addresses look strange when they contain spaces or special characters? Our Free URL Encoder and Decoder is an essential, browser-based utility that translates problematic characters into a universally accepted format called percent-encoding. The internet relies on standard ASCII characters to route traffic and deliver web pages. When you introduce spaces, punctuation marks, or foreign language symbols into a web address, they must be converted into a safe string, such as transforming a simple space into %20. This tool rapidly automates that conversion, making it effortless to encode raw text for web transmission, or to reverse the process by decoding an existing complex link back into highly readable human text. Everything happens instantaneously within your active browser window, ensuring zero latency and maximum convenience.

Key Features

  • Dual-Mode Processing: Easily switch between encoding raw inputs into safe web strings and decoding messy percent-encoded URLs back to plain text.
  • Comprehensive Character Support: Accurately converts spaces, ampersands, equals signs, quotes, and international unicode symbols according to strict web standards.
  • Instant Browser-Based Execution: Results are generated in real time as you paste your input, eliminating the need for page reloads or loading screens.
  • One-Click Clipboard Copy: Accelerate your daily development or marketing workflow by instantly copying the processed output string with a single click.
