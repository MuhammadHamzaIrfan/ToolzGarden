How to Build Your Resume Online for Free

Step 1 — Choose a Template

Select Classic for a traditional, ATS-friendly single-column layout, or Modern for a two-column design with a skills sidebar. The preview updates instantly when you switch.

Step 2 — Fill In Your Details

Complete each section: Personal Information, Professional Summary, Work Experience, Education, and Skills. Required fields are marked with an asterisk (*). The live preview on the right updates as you type.

Step 3 — Download as PDF

Click Download PDF. Your browser's print dialog opens — select Save as PDF as the destination and click Save. Your professional resume is ready to share or submit.

Tips for Writing a Great Resume

  • Keep your summary concise — 2–3 sentences focused on your value proposition
  • Use action verbs in experience descriptions: Led, Developed, Increased, Managed
  • Quantify achievements where possible: “Increased sales by 30%” beats “improved sales”
  • Tailor for each application — match skills and keywords to the job description
  • Keep it to one page if you have less than 10 years of experience
