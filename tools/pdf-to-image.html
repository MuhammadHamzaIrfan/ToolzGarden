How To Use

  1. Locate the file upload zone on the page and drag and drop your target PDF file into it, or click the area to select a file from your device's local folders.
  2. Allow a few seconds for the application to process your document; it will automatically read the PDF and begin rendering each page into a standalone image.
  3. Scroll through the visual preview grid that appears below the upload area to verify that all pages have been converted accurately and the quality meets your expectations.
  4. Click the "Download All Images" button located in the sidebar to immediately save the entire set of converted images to your computer.
  5. If you want to convert a different document, simply click the "Reset Inputs" button to clear the current session and upload a new PDF file.
