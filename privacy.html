Privacy Policy

At PixTools, we take your privacy extremely seriously. Unlike many other online image tools, we do not upload your images to any server.

1. Local Processing

All image processing (compression, resizing, conversion) is performed directly in your web browser using the Canvas API and FileReader API. Your images never leave your computer or device.

2. No Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or share any of the image data you process. We do not have a backend database to store your files.

3. Analytics

We may use basic analytics (like Google Analytics) to understand how users find our site and which tools are most popular. This data is anonymized and does not include any information about your images.

4. Offline Use

Because PixTools works locally, you can even use it without an internet connection once the page has loaded.
