Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 15, 2026

1. Introduction

At ToolzGarden, we take your privacy extremely seriously. This policy covers how we handle, collect, and process data when you use our website and tools.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. We do not store any files you process. We do not have a backend database to store your data, files, or images.

3. How We Use Information

We use anonymous analytics data only to improve the website. This data cannot be linked back to any individual user.

4. File Processing

All file processing happens locally in your browser. Your files are never uploaded to our servers. Because ToolzGarden works locally, your data remains completely private on your own device.

5. Cookies

We use minimal cookies only for theme preference (dark/light mode) to enhance your user experience.

6. Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

7. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our website. This data is anonymous and helps us identify which tools are most popular and where we can improve our services.

8. Third Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party sites that are not operated by us. We have no control over, and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party sites or services.

9. Children's Privacy

ToolzGarden does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with Personal Data, please contact us.

10. Changes To Policy

We may update this policy and will notify users by updating the date below. Your continued use of the Service after any modifications indicates your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

11. Contact
